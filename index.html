About me

My name is Eric Schmidt, I am a software developer based in Brazil.
I have a background in electronic engineering and have worked across several industries and niches, including banking, education technology, SaaS, robotics and electrical engineering.
I also have years of experience as a team leader in a dynamic startup environment, where I improved my leadership and project management skills.
I am passionate about technology and how it can empower people to create value and improve their lives.

Experience in a wide range of companies

As a team leader at Fluency Academy, I played a key role in scaling product launches and managing teams for global operations. I have also contributed to projects as a back-end developer at Bradesco, and as a full-stack developer at Softpar (later acquired by Sinqia).
These diverse experiences have sharpened my technical skills, project management abilities, and adaptability.

Solid electronic engineering background

My bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering gives me a solid understanding of control systems, information processing, operating systems, and mathematics.
This knowledge forms the foundation for efficient problem-solving and system development in software engineering, enabling me to deliver well-rounded solutions.

Worked with a diverse set of technologies

I have hands-on experience with a wide range of technologies, including Python, Java, C, C++, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, MongoDB, SQL, Node.js, Express.js, and Git. I have leveraged these tools to develop robust and scalable applications.

Languages and Communication Skills

I am fluent in both Portuguese and English, and I have intermediate proficiency in German. I have worked in international teams in which English was the only language I used every day.
Also, my experience as a team leader helped me develop strong communication skills to collaborate effectively with stakeholders, colleagues, and clients.
